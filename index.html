About Me

As a native of Atlanta, Ga my journey to adulthood began when I joined the U.S. Navy at 19. I enlisted as a weapons technician and spent almost 4 years on a Navy combat vessal completing two deployments overseas. I learned the importance of teamwork, communication, perseverance, and integrity. Eventually, I became workcenter supervisor and mentor to younger service members. After being honorbably discharged, I used my military benefits to attend college, where I would earn a bachelors of science in computer informations.

It may be of interest to note, I worked full-time as a police officer while attending college full-time. As a law enforcement officer, my critical thinking skills were sharpened, and I learned to deal with persons of all background in good and bad circumstances. When I sucessfully graduated from college, my thirst to create things and design solutions for others became my primary goal. I completed DigitalCrafts' 16-week software developer bootcamp where I learned immersive content about full-stack development. At the moment, I am a software engineer at Syntel, Inc.
